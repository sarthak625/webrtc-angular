🎥 🤙🏻 WebRTC Video Calling

There are two implementations in place here:

➡️ one to one call works with a Firestore signalling server. The caller creates an offer and the joinee provides an answer to establish connection

➡️ A group call relies on a SFU(Selective Forwarding Unit) which is capable of receiving multiple media streams and decides which stream goes to which customer
